Project Information

  • Title: Promotional Transformation Analysis
  • Category: Excel
  • Key Tasks: Data Cleaning, Pivoting, Create Dashboard
  • Project URL: github

Project Objectives

The purpose of this analysis is to evaluate the effectiveness and profitability of various promotional strategies implemented during the 2025 period. This analysis focuses on identifying anomalies and inefficiencies to provide data-driven recommendations to transform the company's commercial strategy going forward.

Findings

Worst Promotion Type

The "Buy 1 Get 1" promotion was the only one that was detrimental to the company, with a total loss of -Rp 34.6 million. Despite this, this promotion sold a significant number of units (9,688 units), albeit with a negative average profit margin (-5%).

Best Promotion Type

Discount-based promotions were the most profitable among the other options. The "15% Discount" contributed the highest profit, at IDR 611.5 million, followed by the "20% Cashback" (IDR 458.5 million), and the "10% Discount" (IDR 381 million).

Revenue Trends Show Decline in Second Half

Monthly revenue trends showed strong performance in Q1, peaking in March at Rp 813.4 million. However, a sharp decline began in July, a -69% decline from June's Rp 245.9 million, and remained low through the end of the year.

Performance Varies Between Units

There is a significant difference in performance between stores with the store with the highest profit being STORE-BDG-02 (Rp 600.8 million). The application of promotion types between units has interesting findings, namely showing that in STORE-MKS-04 the Buy 1 Get 1 promotion has a fairly promising profit of Rp 21.3 million.

Data Quality

There were significant data completeness issues, particularly in the "Channel_Penjualan" (Sales_Channel column). A total of Rp 537.8 million could not be allocated to any channel because the data was recorded as "Tidak Diketahui" (Unknown) or previously null.

Reccomendation

Stop the "Buy 1 Get 1" Promotion Immediately

Reallocate the budget from this promotion to other promotions that are proven to be profitable such as "Diskon 15%" (15% Off) or "Bundling".

Learn How to Improve the Performance of "Buy 1 Get 1" Promotions

Look at the store units that have promising profits on this promotion, and apply it to other stores.

Prioritize Percentage-Based Promotions

Focus the commercial strategy on “15% Off”, “10% Off”, and “20% Cashback” promotions that have healthy profitability and margins.

Improve Data Input Process

Implement mandatory field validation in the system to eliminate "Unknown" data or null values.

Conduct Advanced Analysis

Conduct a more in-depth investigation to understand the cause of the drastic revenue decline that began in July 2025.
