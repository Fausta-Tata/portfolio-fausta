Project Information

  • Title: Student Performance Analysis
  • Category: PowerBI
  • Key Tasks: Create Measure and Dashboard

Project Objectives

The purpose of this analysis is to evaluate the effectiveness and profitability of various promotional strategies implemented during the 2025 period. This analysis focuses on identifying anomalies and inefficiencies to provide data-driven recommendations to transform the company's commercial strategy going forward.

Findings

Correlation Between Study Time and Final Grade

It was found that students who had high study hours also had high final grades.

The Impact of Social Interaction on Grade

There is a slight decrease in grades for students who have romantic relationships and also often go out with friends.

Demographic and Background Factors

Family relationships significantly influence student grades. However, no notable performance differences were found between male and female students.

Reccomendation

Developing an Academic Support Program

Given the influence of study time on academic performance, it is recommended to:

  • Implement after-school Guided Study Groups to improve study habits.
  • Provide targeted tutoring for students with previous course failures (failures >0) as early intervention.

Improving Student Balance

To balance the negative impacts of excessive social activity, it is recommended to:

  • Increase the variety and promotion of positive extracurricular activities as an alternative to structured social activities within the school environment.

Strengthening Family Relationships & Support

Given the strong influence of the quality of family relationships (famrel) on student grades, it is recommended to:

  • Hold seminars or workshops for parents that focus on how to build supportive family relationships to create an effective learning environment at home.
